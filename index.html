Research and Projects


Robot Teleoperation Exoskeleton

Research project and senior project to develop a novel 1-to-1 teleoperation control scheme for robotic appendages and humanoid robots. Central objective is to achieve a working kinematic control scheme while utilizing lower cost components and achieving equal or greater accuracy when compared to contemporary methods of robotic teleoperation.

Tools and Languages Used: C++, C, Autodesk Fusion, Python

T-COM Project

Designing PCBs and hardware for novel mHealth device, slated for future clinical testing. Developed prototyping PCBs and final board incorporating a PSoC 6 MCU, CO sensor, 3G modem, audio section, and TFT capacitive touch display. Primary student author of a paper for submission to the 2020 IEEE EMBS Biomedical & Health International Conference and journal.

Tools and Languages Used: Autodesk Eagle CAD, C

Force Augmenting Hand Exoskeleton

Developing PCB and associated hardware for robotic hand exoskeleton, utilizing six linear actuators to augment grip strength in conjunction with force-sensing resistors. Project intended to help individuals with reduced grip strength due to age/illness.

Tools and Languages Used: C++, Solidworks

Petri Net Robotic Research Project

Research project incorporating fuzzy petri nets to create algorithim for predicting and controlling humanoid robotic appendages.

Tools and Languages Used: Fuzzy Petri Nets, C++, Python

SERVD

Contributed to the SERVD platform, a Ruby on Rails based web app for student volunteering and outreach at The College of New Jersey. Added login and event functionalities to existing framework, and helped re-implement PostgresSQL database.

Tools and Languages Used: Ruby on Rails, PostgresSQL, HTML, CSS, Javascript

3DoF Robot Arm

Robot arm demo for TCF 2020 Presentation. Designed and programmed simple 3DoF arm to follow direct input and perform pre-programmed movements.

Tools and Languages Used: C, Autodesk Fusion, PSoC Environment
